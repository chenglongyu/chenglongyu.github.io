Syllabus: This is the second half of a year long course in algebra, with emphasis on topics concerning group representations, fields and Galois theory. We will cover bilinear forms (chapter 8), group representations (chapter 10), basics on fields and Galois theory (chapter 15, 16).

The goal of the class is to develop intuition from concrete examples and apply the power of abstraction to solve problems.
