I am an assistant professor at YMSC and BIMSA from Nov 2020. I was a Hans Rademacher Instructor of Mathematics at University of Pennsylvania. I graduated from Harvard University in 2018, under the supervision of Shing-Tung Yau

My research is in algebraic geometry and differential geometry. I am interested in the geometry and arithmetic properties of Calabi-Yau varieties, especially the differential systems arising from Calabi-Yau families. I am also interested in moduli of K3 surfaces and cubic fourfolds.

Papers

  1. Moduli of Singular Sextic Curves via Periods of K3 Surfaces. (with Zhiwei Zheng)
  2. Moduli Spaces of Symmetric Cubic Fourfolds and Locally Symmetric Varieties. (with Zhiwei Zheng)
  3. Jacobian rings for homogenous vector bundles and applications. (with An Huang, Bong Lian, Shing-Tung Yau)
  4. Period integrals of local complete intersections and tautological systems. (with An Huang, Bong Lian, Shing-Tung Yau)
  5. Hasse-Witt matrices, unit roots and period integrals. (with An Huang, Bong Lian, Shing-Tung Yau)
